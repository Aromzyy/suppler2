Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs)

How does the app detect skin conditions?

Our app uses advanced machine learning algorithms trained on a diverse dataset of skin conditions. By analyzing images of the skin condition, it predicts possible conditions based on patterns recognized during its training.

Is the app accurate?

While our app is highly accurate and continuously improving, it's designed to offer preliminary information and cannot replace a professional medical diagnosis. We recommend consulting with a dermatologist for an accurate diagnosis.

How do I use the app?

Simply upload a clear picture of the affected skin area through our app. The machine learning model will analyze the image and provide you with a list of possible conditions, including information about them.

What about my privacy?

We prioritize user privacy. Images and data provided to the app are encrypted and used solely for condition detection purposes. We do not share your data without your explicit consent.
